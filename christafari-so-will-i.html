So Will I (100 Billion X) Hillsong Cover
[Feat. Avion Blackman] Music Video

In this project I had the oportunity to make all the chroma keys and integrate them with backgrounds and textures using a very nice technique called Double Exposure. I also made all the trackings, integration, final compositing, art and Motion graphics.
